Payment via Credit / Debit Card

  • Orders paid for by credit / debit cards incur a fee of 1.5% (inc. GST) on the total cost.
  • Travelex will not charge a cash advance fee for orders paid for by credit /debit card, but your card issuer may. The value and application of this fee is determined by your card issuer.
  • If these orders are to be collected from an airport location, a minimum of 1 Business Day* is required for the order to be processed.
  • Orders collected from a non-airport location take a minimum of 3 Business Days* to be processed.

Payment via BPAY?

  • Orders paid for via BPAY incur no additional fees.
  • BPAY orders collected from an airport location take a minimum of 3 Business Days* to be processed.
  • BPAY orders collected from a non-airport location take a minimum of 5 Business Days* to be processed.

*Business Day means Monday to Friday excluding National Public Holidays and Bank Holidays. For orders made after 3pm on a Business Day, an additional Business Day is required to ensure the order is ready for collection.
