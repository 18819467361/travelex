Frequently Asked Questions

Online Offers

Please refer to our Online Ordering Service Terms and Conditions for further information. If there is any inconsistency between this Frequently Asked Questions section and the Online Ordering Service Terms and Conditions, the terms of the Online Ordering Service Terms and Conditions should prevail to the extent of the inconsistency.

Can I buy foreign currencies online?

No. Currently you can only reserve your foreign currency banknotes (“Travel Money”) online for collection. Payment will be taken when you collect your Travel Money in-store.

How long can I place an order in advance?

You shall place your order 2-5 days in advance of your selected collection date.

Your order must be received by us by noon (12pm) local time for us to meet the minimum turnaround time. We will use our reasonable endeavors to meet the minimum turnaround time, but we will not be liable for failing to do so.

How much foreign currency can I reserve per order?

All online orders are subject to:

a) a minimum value of RMB 1,500 (or the equivalent in other currencies) per order; and

b) a maximum value of RMB 20,000 (or the equivalent in other currencies) per order for mainland Chinese residents or RMB 6,000 (or the equivalent in other currencies) per order for Other Residents.

These order limits are subject to change from time to time at Travelex's sole discretion. For the purpose of these Terms and Conditions, the term ‘Other Residents’ means residents of Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan and residents of foreign countries.

What is the maximum annual limit I can exchange annually?

Mainland Chinese resident may convert foreign currency into RMB up to USD$50,000 equivalent annually. Conversion from RMB into foreign currency is up to USD50, 000 equivalent annually.

Other residents may only convert foreign currencies into RMB up to US$50,000 equivalent annually. Conversion from RMB to foreign currencies is not restricted and no annual limit applies.

Above rules are for reference only. Quota and limit restrictions are subject to change according to updates from State Administration of Foreign Exchange of PRC and its regional offices.

Can I request for specific denominations?

No. Requests for specific denominations are not currently available through our on-line reservation service as the denominations are dependent on what our stores have in stock.

Can I change the pick-up date of my order?

No. You cannot amend the pick-up date of your order. Please cancel the order instead by calling the store number listed on your confirmation email.

Can I cancel my order?

You may choose to cancel your reservation at anytime before your selected collection date by calling the store number listed on your confirmation email.

Which stores can I reserve currency for collection at?

You can find the available collection stores on our online ordering screen.

Will I get the rates shown on your website?

Yes. The rates quoted on the website when you click the "Reservation" button will be locked-in and be valid for up to 5 days from the date of the order.

 

How is the exchange rate for your online purchase determined ?

It is simple, simply select the currency you require and tell us the store you’d like to collect your currency from. In our order confirmation email to you, we’ll give you locked-in rate. The exchange rate applicable to your actual Travel Money purchase is either:

a. the exchange rate set out in the confirmation email provided to you by us; or

b. the exchange rate displayed on the rate board or other exchange rates notified by our staff at the Collection Store at the time of collection.

You may choose one of the above exchange rates whichever is more preferable for you.

 

What if I want more foreign currency?

You may increase the amount of Travel Money you require when collecting your Travel Money. Please discuss further details with our staff.

 

How do I find out more about Travelex?

The best source of information is our website. To visit the corporate site, please click here.

I’d like to work for Travelex, how do I find out about vacancies?

Visit our employment section.

Security Statement

Fraudulent emails

Travelex has become aware of a number of attempted email frauds, in which sellers on online auction sites receive an email claiming to be from Travelex and containing the Travelex logo, requesting that sellers make a cash transfer or ship their goods, in advance of payment. In some cases, these emails also request personal information from sellers such as name, address, etc. Should you receive such an email, please disregard it. Travelex will never request that anyone make a payment in order to facilitate a further payment from a buyer to a seller.

Travelex is also aware of an email fraud which involves offers of fake employment or training opportunities with Travelex. Please note this is not a legitimate Travelex initiative and is an attempt to obtain personal information from individuals including bank account details which will be compromised and used for illegal purposes. Under no circumstances should you provide any personal information including bank account details to these fraudsters who have no connection whatsoever with the Travelex organisation.

If you have provided your banking information you should report this immediately to your banking institution. Legitimate Travelex e-mails will only come from @travelex.com.cn @travelex.com.au @apac.travelex.com or @travelex.com addresses, and we will never ask you for your bank details via email.

Travelex has notified the relevant law enforcement authorities of both frauds.

Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer's hard drive by your browser. The information collected by "cookies" does not identify you as an individual. They help us improve the Website and make it possible for Travelex to identify some of your preferences. On revisiting the Website, Travelex's computer server will recognise the cookie, giving us information about your previous visits.

Most browsers are originally configured to accept cookies automatically, however, you may refuse to accept cookies by activating the setting on your browser which allows you to refuse the setting of cookies. Please note that if your browser is not configured properly or if you alter your browser to disable the acceptance of cookies, you may not be able to use the Website. Unless you have adjusted your browser settings so that it will refuse cookies, our system will automatically issue cookies when you log on to the Website.
